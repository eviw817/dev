Challenge 3

For the third challenge, we had to build a recognitionapp using webcam-easy.

The recognitionapp goes as following:

  1. You follow the link down below.
  2. You follow the instructions:
    • You allow the website to access your camera.
    • You wait for a little while until you can see yourself. You'll be shown in a mirror-perspective.
    • Press the "capture" button.
    • The app scans the picture and guesses what's in it!
  3. The last rule is: enjoy!
